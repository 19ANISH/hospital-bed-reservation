Frequently Asked Questioins

Some of the most frequently asked to us about our website.

  • Do we need an account to reserve our bed in any hospital?

    No, patient or user doesn't require any kind of account. You can directly fill the details and confirm them before booking.

  • What happens if a patient reserves a bed and then don't contact to hospital in time?

    In such cases hospital can use that particular bed for another patient if one doesn't contact hospital in time.

  • How to confirm that bed reservation is done successfully?

    You will recieve an email regarding your reservation with details you have entered and the same details will be forwarded to hospital for their confirmation as well.

  • What if after reservation a hospital doesn't alot you the bed?

    Patient can contact the admin and tell him about the incident, then admin will act accordingly either the hospital will be removed from the website listing and if required severe measures will be taken.
